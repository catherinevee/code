Frequently Asked Questions

Gaming consoles and smart TVs require device registration. Visit the IT Help Desk with your device's MAC address, or use our online device registration portal at wifi.sunflower.edu/register.

Dorm WiFi speed can be affected by:

  • Peak usage times (evenings and weekends)
  • Distance from access points
  • Building construction materials
  • Too many devices connected

Try moving closer to common areas or use ethernet cables for the best speeds.
