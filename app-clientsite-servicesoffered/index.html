Library Services

Comprehensive facilities and services designed to support academic research and study

High-Speed Internet & WiFi

Complimentary high-speed internet access throughout the facility with dedicated research bandwidth. Secure network with VPN access for remote database connectivity.

Library Café

Our on-site café serves freshly brewed coffee, specialty teas, and light meals. Open daily from 7:30 AM, with both quiet study zones and collaborative spaces.

Study Spaces

Over 200 individual study carrels, 15 group study rooms (2-8 people), silent reading rooms, and collaborative workspaces with whiteboard access.

Research Support

Professional librarians available for research consultations, database training, citation assistance, and specialized subject expertise.

Technology Services

Computer workstations, printing and scanning services, laptop lending program, and presentation practice rooms with AV equipment.

Extended Access

Late-night hours until 11 PM on weekdays, with 24/7 keycard access available for graduate students and faculty researchers.

Book Collections & Resources

Extensive academic collections spanning multiple disciplines with both physical and digital resources

Collection Overview

Our library houses over 50,000 volumes across six major academic disciplines, plus extensive digital archives and database access. All materials are available for on-site use, with lending privileges for registered users.
